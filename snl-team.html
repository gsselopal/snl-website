Dr. G.S. Selopal

Canada Research Chair (Tier 2) · Associate Professor
Department of Engineering, Faculty of Agriculture, Dalhousie University

Dr. Selopal leads the Sustainable Nanoengineering Lab with expertise in nanostructured materials for solar energy conversion, hydrogen production, and environmental remediation. His work has been recognized nationally through NSERC funding and the prestigious Canada Research Chair program.
