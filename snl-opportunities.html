Graduate Students

We are actively seeking motivated graduate students with backgrounds in chemistry, engineering, materials science, or physics to pursue MSc or PhD research.

  • Strong academic record (A- or better)
  • Background in chemistry, physics, or engineering
  • Motivation for research
  • English proficiency (international applicants)
Funding Available: NSERC CGS, FGS Harmonized Scholarship, Dal Graduate Awards

Undergraduate Students

Gain hands-on research experience in nanotechnology. We offer summer research positions (NSERC USRA) and year-round opportunities for high-achieving undergrads.

  • NSERC USRA eligibility
  • Summer and academic year positions
  • Hands-on lab experience
  • Mentorship from experienced researchers
Funding Available: NSERC USRA, MITACS Globalink, Dalhousie SURA
