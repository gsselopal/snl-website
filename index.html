Research Pillars

Four interconnected research areas driving innovation in nanotechnology for sustainable solutions.

Photoelectrochemical Solar Energy

Converting sunlight into electricity using nanostructured photoelectrodes. Advancing sustainable energy generation through advanced materials.

Energy

Green Hydrogen Production

Sustainable hydrogen via photoelectrochemical water splitting. Developing clean fuel sources for the future economy.

Hydrogen

Precision Agriculture & Nano-Sensors

Nanomaterial-based sensors for smart farming. Enabling data-driven agriculture for improved crop yields and resource efficiency.

Agriculture

Environmental Remediation

Photocatalytic nanoparticles for pollutant degradation. Cleaning water and air through advanced nanomaterial applications.

Environment
